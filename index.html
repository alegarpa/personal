Who am I?

This is still under construction as you'll probably notice from all the dead-links at the top of the navbar but I am having fun with this. It just feels oddly narcissistic but hopefully I'll get over that. At the end of the day if I want a job I need to sell myself. And probably get over my infatuation with hotpink.

My name is Alejandro Garcia-Paredes and I was born and raised in Victorville, CA. It's a lot different than the Bay area in that there isn't a lot of water and a lot of people live there because it's cheap. There isn't much to the town just people trying to scrape by. Oddly enough in my years there I did witness white flight happen first hand and I came to the realization that every race is racist not just white people, but that's another issue for another time.

I graduated from the University of California Berkeley this past August with a degree with Computer Science. It came with a lot of fighting and I wasn't always sure I was going to finish but I hate quitting and I really wasn't going to let an operating systems class or a compilers class come between me and graduating college. My parents never finished High School and I always had to work 15 hours a week during school, but at the end of the day I figured if I didn't make it those would be excuses and why push yourself so hard without getting a result. It was a enjoyable experience and I really appreciate being pushed and meeting so many intelligent people, I definetly wouldn't have gotten that back home. I like making programs and I love problem solving, and now that I have time to make that my main focus I'm really content.

I'm currently Interning at Agribots and that's been a lot of fun. I just want to learn as much as humanely possible so I can be of some use to making the world a better place.
