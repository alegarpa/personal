Who am I?

I can't expect a lot of traffic right now and the page is still literally vanilla. I'll see how things go haha. I'm not sure what the purpose of this will be long-term. I should get better at math also hahaha. Maybe make some youtube videos. Etc. Etc. I just need some direction.

My name is Alejandro Garcia-Paredes and I was born and raised in Victorville, CA. It's a lot different than the Bay area in that there isn't a lot of water and a lot of people live there because it's cheap. There isn't much to the town just people trying to scrape by. A lot like me right now. Maybe I only know how to struggle and maybe it's not the best thing that I pressure myself so much. I just feel like my family is counting on me, and I am literaly the only person I know who studied computer science at CAL who doesn't have a job yet.

I graduated from the University of California Berkeley this past August with a degree with Computer Science. It came with a lot of fighting and I wasn't always sure I was going to finish but I hate quitting and I really wasn't going to let an operating systems class or a compilers class come between me and graduating college. My parents never finished High School and I always had to work 15 hours a week during school, but at the end of the day I figured if I didn't make it those would be excuses and why push yourself so hard without getting a result. It was a enjoyable experience and I really appreciate being pushed and meeting so many intelligent people, I definetly wouldn't have gotten that back home. I like making programs and I love problem solving, and now that I have time to make that my main focus I'm really content.
